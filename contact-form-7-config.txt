CONTACT FORM 7 CONFIGURATION FOR SECURITY TRAINING ACADEMY THEME
================================================================

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Install and activate the Contact Form 7 plugin
2. Go to Contact > Contact Forms in your WordPress admin
3. Create a new form or edit an existing one
4. Copy and paste the form code below into the Form tab
5. Copy and paste the mail settings into the Mail tab
6. Copy and paste the CSS into your theme's style.css or Additional CSS
7. Use the shortcode [contact-form-7 id="YOUR_FORM_ID" title="Contact form"] in the contact-section.php

FORM CODE (Copy this into the Form tab):
========================================

<div class="cf7-form-grid">
    <div class="cf7-field-group">
        [text* your-name class:cf7-input placeholder "Your Name"]
    </div>
    
    <div class="cf7-field-group">
        [email* your-email class:cf7-input placeholder "Your Email"]
    </div>
    
    <div class="cf7-field-group">
        [tel your-phone class:cf7-input placeholder "Your Phone"]
    </div>
    
    <div class="cf7-field-group cf7-field-full">
        [textarea* your-message class:cf7-textarea placeholder "Your Message"]
    </div>
    
    <div class="cf7-field-group cf7-field-full">
        [submit class:cf7-submit "Send Message"]
    </div>
</div>

SHORTCODE TO USE:
================
[contact-form-7 id="YOUR_FORM_ID" title="Contact form"]

Replace YOUR_FORM_ID with the actual ID of your contact form.

NOTES:
======
- Make sure to update email addresses to match your domain
- Test the form after setup to ensure emails are being sent
- Consider setting up SMTP for better email delivery
- The form is fully responsive and matches your theme's design
- All fields except phone are required by default
- Form includes proper validation and error handling
